🔗 Google Sheets Connection

Paste your Apps Script Web App URL below. This connects the app to your Google Sheet so items and stock counts sync automatically.

📋 Setup Instructions

  1. Download the HomeInventory_GoogleSheet_Template.xlsx file and upload it to Google Drive. Right-click → Open with → Google Sheets.
  2. In Google Sheets, click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete the existing code, paste in the HomeInventory_AppsScript.js file contents, and click Save.
  3. Click Deploy → New deployment. Set type to Web app, Execute as Me, Who has access Anyone. Click Deploy and authorise with your Google account.
  4. Copy the Web App URL from the deployment screen, paste it into the field above, and tap Connect.
  5. The app will load your item list from the Sheet. Any items added or changed in the Sheet will appear next time the app loads.

👤 Your Name (for sweep log)

This name appears in the Sweep History tab of the Google Sheet so the Administrator knows who completed each check.

⚠️ Reset Data

This clears all locally saved stock counts. Does not affect the Google Sheet. Use if you want to start a fresh sweep from scratch.
